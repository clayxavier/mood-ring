MOOD RING

Life isnt about feeling happy all the time. It's healthy to have ups and downs. Just remember to reach out when you're stuck. Mood Ring is here to help!


"The Mood Ring app could be a great way for young adolescents to learn that feelings are transient and that 'this too shall pass."

Stacy Sterling, DrPH, MSW | Staff Scientist at Kaiser

Mood Ring was created by Youth Radio in partnership with MIT App Inventor, supported in part by the National Science Foundation and Kaiser Permanente Northern California Community Benefit Programs.
